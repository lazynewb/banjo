Your Journey

Prepare for an extraordinary journey through Indonesia's most breathtaking islands. Our meticulously planned itinerary ensures you'll experience the best of Bali, the Nusa Islands, Lombok, Sumbawa, and the iconic Komodo Island. Get ready for stunning landscapes, vibrant marine life, and encounters with incredible wildlife.

Bali to the Nusa Islands

Our adventure begins as we set sail from Bali, crossing the azure waters to the enchanting Nusa Lembongan. From there, we journey onward to Nusa Penida, a captivating island ready to be explored.

Lombok & Gili Trawangan

We escape to the serene island of Lombok, then journey directly to the vibrant shores of Gili Trawangan. Dive into an underwater paradise with a refreshing snorkeling session, or simply enjoy the island's laid-back charm during your free time.

Peaceful Sumbawa

The journey continues to Kenawa Island, a true hidden gem. Embark on a short trek to the hilltop for breathtaking panoramic views of Lombok and Sumbawa, or capture stunning photos amidst the expansive grassy fields below. As the day draws to a close, our boat glides towards the tranquil waters of Saleh Bay for a peaceful overnight stay.

Sumbawa's Gentle Giants

Experience an unforgettable morning in Saleh Bay, West Sumbawa, where you'll have the incredible opportunity to snorkel alongside majestic whale sharks. Afterward, our voyage continues eastward, charting a course for the legendary Komodo Islands.

Flores: Sea of Wonders

Wake up to the wonders of Komodo Island, where you'll embark on a thrilling trek to witness the ancient Komodo dragons in their natural habitat, guided by an expert ranger. Next, we journey to the iconic Pink Beach for an unforgettable snorkeling experience or simply to relax on its unique rosy sands. As evening approaches, we ascend to the summit of Padar Island for a truly spectacular sunset vista.

Komodo Island Treasures

Our final day unfolds with an invigorating snorkeling adventure at Manjerite, revealing a vibrant underwater world of colorful corals. Then, we venture to Kelor Island for a rewarding trek, immersing ourselves in its natural beauty.

Facility

When you book with us, you can relax knowing almost everything is taken care of! We've thoughtfully designed our inclusions to ensure a seamless and unforgettable journey.

Effortless Transfers

From the moment you arrive at our meeting point, we've got your transportation covered. Enjoy comfortable, round-trip shuttle service to and from the harbor, so you can focus on the adventure ahead.

Comfortable Experience

Your home for six incredible days will be our well-appointed live-aboard boat, setting sail every two weeks for your convenience. Whether you choose the cozy Deck Class or the private Cabin Class, you'll have a comfortable space to relax and recharge after a day of exploration.

Delicious Dining

Get ready to indulge in 15 delicious meals prepared right on board! Our chefs create varied menus, and we're happy to accommodate your requests to ensure every meal is a delight. Plus, stay refreshed with unlimited mineral water, coffee, and tea throughout your journey.

Hassle-Free Activities

Forget about hidden fees! All your access is sorted, including parking, tolls, and entrance fees for all tourist attractions, most notably the magnificent Komodo National Park. We also provide full snorkeling gear, so you're ready to dive into the vibrant underwater world whenever the opportunity arises.

Safety is Our Priority

Travel with peace of mind knowing your safety is paramount. We include a reliable speedboat tender for comfortable excursions to various spots and keep a comprehensive first aid kit on board, ensuring you're well looked after every step of the way.
